Privacy Policy for Mancave Snooker

Last updated: 14 June 2026

Mancave Snooker is a snooker scoreboard app. We only collect the limited information needed to let you create players, generate optional cartoon avatars, and keep score.

1. Who we are

Mancave Snooker is operated by Eno Solutions. For privacy questions, you can contact us at: privacy@eno.solutions.

2. Information we collect

When you use Mancave Snooker, we may collect and process the following information:

  • Player names entered into the app.
  • Cartoon avatar images created for players.
  • Match and score data, including player scores and match history.
  • Selfies or uploaded photos when you choose to create a cartoon avatar.

Mancave Snooker does not require you to create an account or log in.

3. Cartoon avatar creation

If you choose to create a cartoon avatar, the photo or selfie you provide is sent securely to OpenAI so that a cartoon-style avatar can be generated.

We do not store the original selfie or uploaded photo after the avatar has been created. We only store the resulting cartoon avatar so it can be shown in the app.

Avatar creation is optional. You can use the scoreboard features without uploading a photo.

4. How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Create and display players in the app.
  • Generate and display cartoon avatars.
  • Record and display snooker scores and match information.
  • Operate, maintain, and improve the app.

5. Where your information is stored

Player names, cartoon avatars, and score data may be stored using our backend systems, including Cloudflare services.

Original uploaded selfies or photos are not stored by us once the avatar creation process has completed.

6. Third-party services

Mancave Snooker uses the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI — used to generate cartoon avatars from photos you choose to upload.
  • Cloudflare — used to host and operate parts of the app backend.
  • Apple — used to distribute the app through TestFlight and the App Store.

These third parties may process data in accordance with their own privacy and security terms.

7. Analytics and tracking

Mancave Snooker does not use third-party analytics, advertising trackers, or crash reporting tools.

8. Legal basis for processing

For UK data protection purposes, we process your information because it is necessary to provide the app features you choose to use, and because we have a legitimate interest in operating and maintaining the app.

Where you choose to upload a selfie or photo for avatar creation, you are choosing to provide that image for the purpose of generating your avatar.

9. How long we keep your information

We keep player names, cartoon avatars, and score data for as long as needed to provide the app’s features, unless you ask us to delete them.

Original selfies or uploaded photos are not stored by us after the avatar has been generated.

10. Your rights

If you are in the UK, you have rights under UK data protection law. These may include the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate information.
  • Ask us to delete your information.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Complain to the UK Information Commissioner’s Office if you are unhappy with how your data is handled.

To make a request, contact us at privacy@eno.solutions.

11. Children’s privacy

Mancave Snooker is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

12. Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect the information processed by the app. However, no internet-based service can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

13. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.

14. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, contact: privacy@eno.solutions.

This policy is intended for users in the United Kingdom.
