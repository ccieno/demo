Description
  • Liver Cleanse Complex is a unique combination of choline, milk thistle, artichoke, and turmeric for detoxification, liver health, digestion, and cholestero balance
  • Specific solution - One of our edited range of go-to specific solution products for your health concerns
  • Effective -Choline is an essential phospholipid which supports liver function and lipid metabolism, whilst artichoke and milk thistle are potent herbs which aid digestion (especially of fats), detoxification, liver and gallbladder health
  • Optimum support - With turmeric which prevents the accumulation of fats in the liver and has anti-oxidant and anti-inflammatory properties, and broccoli, is a rich source of micronutrients, including sulphoraphane, glucaric acid, and sulphur
  • Advanced - Milk thistle, artichoke and broccoli are provided as highly-concentrated extracts with standardised amounts of active constituents for maximum effectiveness
  • Comprehensive - Also contains taurine for cholesterol balance alongside Trimethylglycine (TMG), N-acetyl cysteine, and alpha lipoic acid
  • Synergistic formulation - Provides a range of specifically selected nutrients and plant extracts for broad-spectrum and balanced detoxification and liver support
  • Flexible - Easy to swallow capsules allowing convenience and splitting dose throughout the day ensures maximum efficacy
  • No unnecessary additives
  • Environmentally friendly - Recyclable packaging
  • Trusted - We've been helping to shape a healthier society for over 30 years
  • Suitable for vegetarians and vegans
